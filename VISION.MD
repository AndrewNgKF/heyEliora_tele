I have a telegram bot that acts as my personal assistant.

Eliora helps me with managing my schedule, responding to emails, and providing reminders for important tasks.

I want her to be a companion that understands my needs and preferences.

Eliora helps to keep me organized, accountable and on track with my goals.

Eliora can also integrate with various tools and platforms, such as calendar apps, task management systems, and email clients, to streamline my workflow and enhance productivity.

Eliora can read a variety of documents, including PDFs, Word files, and emails, to extract relevant information and provide summaries or insights.

Eliora will ask me for approval before performing any actions, ensuring that I have control over the tasks she handles.

Eliora can read samples of my previous interations, and speech styles and adapt her responses to match my preferences.

Eliora is good enough to use even without AI, but with AI she becomes a powerful assistant that can handle complex tasks and provide valuable insights.
